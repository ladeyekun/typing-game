Instruction

To play the game, click on the 'START' button. You will see a word and you are expected to type the word in correctly to proceed to another word.

For every correctly typed work, you will earn a hit.

The game ends either when the game timed out or when you typed in all available words. You can restart the game before timing out by clicking on the 'RESTART' button, and this will reset the timer and hits counter.

You are only able to view the scores when there is no active play. Click on 'VIEW SCORES' button to see the top nine scores.
